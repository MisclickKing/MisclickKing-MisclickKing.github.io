Requirements & Design Proposal Webpage

Project Overview

This site will be an overhaul of my mother's current business website. Due to some troubles with her last website host, my mom has asked if I would design her website to include the designs and funcitons she is looking for. Her site is a promotion of her hand made garments, made for dancers who do ministry at church and church events. Her products include premade garments and custom ones per the client's request. The site is aimed at people who do dance ministry, regaurdless of age or experience levels.

The site will contain a variety of content, including a logo, an introduction to the site, a newletter sign up, a contact section, a store front page with a list of products, and a gallary of images from past sells and events, among other things. I plan to update this site beyond the scope of this assingnment as well.

Page Design

Home Page

This is an introduction to the main site and what it is about. Customers or those who happen upon the site will find the title, logo, image of the owner of the site, and a series of branching links to futher explore the site. This page will have hyperlinks and a navagation bar. This page is a show and tell intro without specail functions.

Shop All

This webpage will have a list of images/products for sale, as well as an in or out of stock subtext for the product. It will give you the option of check out and a final price listing at the cart, assumping I get that far in the overall process of being about to make a purchase within the site.

Gallery

A series of images of previous works and events, as well as specific sections for gender and age related product viewings. Haven't fully commited to how I want to display these images yet, but I do want them to maximize to full size when clicked on.

Newletter

This section will have news on current or upcoming products and/or events for the customer to partake in. This includes sales and live evetns where the owner might set up a booth for product sales. There will also be a newsletter sign up for getting these newsletter directly sent to your email. This will include a sign up sheet with a require email address.

Contact

This part of the site is for contacting the owner directly for custom orders, questions, or general communications. This section will require filling in information, such as an first and last name, email, and a message for the owner to read.

Dynamic Functionality

I want to incorperate a sense of movement throughout the website. I want to achieve this through small details such as scroll images in gallery and moving UI elements. This was best demonstrated by the site I used as a good example of webdesign in a previous assignment early on in the semester.
